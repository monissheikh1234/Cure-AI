Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data safe on this platform?

Yes! We use end-to-end encryption to protect your medical history. Your data is stored securely and is only accessible to you and those you choose to share it with.

Can I share my records with my doctor?

Yes, you can easily share your medical records with your healthcare provider. Our platform allows you to generate secure links or PDFs that you can share with your doctors.

Is this platform free to use?

We offer both free and premium plans based on your storage needs. The free plan includes basic features, while the premium plan offers additional storage and advanced features.

How do I upload my medical records?

You can upload your medical records by scanning them or taking photos. Our platform also supports direct uploads from your computer or mobile device.
